Kablammo — Watch Face

A Wear OS watch face that animates the Kablammo variable font's axis — in a format (WFF) that has no variable-font animation.

2026 · wip · Solo build

Play / visit ↗

Wear OSAndroidTypographyVariable fonts

Kablammo is a display font with a wiggly variable axis — and Wear OS’s Watch Face Format has no way to animate a font axis. This watch face fakes it anyway, driving the letterforms so the time appears to wobble and pop.
